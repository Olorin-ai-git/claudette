# Privacy Policy — Claudette Mobile

**Last updated:** February 21, 2026

## Overview

Claudette Mobile is an SSH terminal client that connects directly to your own Mac. The app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to us or any third party.

## Data Collection

Claudette Mobile collects **no data**. Specifically:

- No analytics or tracking
- No crash reporting sent to us
- No advertising identifiers
- No personal information collected
- No usage data transmitted

## Data Storage

All data remains on your device:

- **Server profiles** (hostnames, usernames, ports) are stored locally on your device
- **SSH keys** are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device
- **Passwords** are stored in the iOS Keychain on your device
- **Snippets and preferences** are stored locally on your device

No data is synced to any cloud service by the app.

## Network Connections

The app connects **only** to SSH servers that you manually configure. All connections are:

- Direct from your device to your server
- Encrypted via SSH protocol
- Initiated only by you

No other network connections are made. The app does not contact our servers or any third-party services.

## Third-Party Services

Claudette Mobile uses no third-party SDKs, analytics, or services.

## Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.
